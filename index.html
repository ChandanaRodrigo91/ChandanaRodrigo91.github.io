About Me

25+Years of Experience

I am a senior technology leader with a proven track record of shaping IT strategy at enterprise scale. Across three decades spanning retail, global aviation, and financial services, I have led large-scale digital transformation programmes, governed complex multi-site technology operations, and built high-performing teams from the ground up — consistently aligning technology investment with business outcomes and long-term organisational growth.

I bring a distinctive ability to operate at both the strategic and operational levels: partnering with C-suite and global executives to define technology vision while simultaneously driving execution excellence on the ground.
